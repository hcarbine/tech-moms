Portfolio

I loved learning so many new things by becoming a tech-mom! My portfolio consists of screen shots of some of the assignments we did in class. I also included screen shots of my Canvas pages from when I was a teacher. I included these to showcase that I love adding design elements. Elementary teachers love to make things cute. While I enjoyed changing my Canvas home page each month to change things up, I loved seeing my students get so excited when a new home page was posted. Because of hybrid learning, I had students both in class and a few at home. Having everyone access our class resources and assignments in the same way was invaluable. I look forward to all my future projects!
